  • Consolidate dual domains with 301 redirect Developer: Set up 301 permanent redirect from alaskasbestlodge.com → sportsmanscovelodge.com. This tells Google that all authority flows to the primary domain. Check within 7 days using Google Search Console to verify redirect is working. Update all external links (Google My Business, directories, social bios, email signatures) to use sportsmanscovelodge.com only.

  • Publish 8 additional blog posts (2 per week) Topics: Fishing techniques by species, destination guides (Ketchikan, Prince of Wales Island), trip planning guides, guest stories (blog post featuring a past guest's experience), crew/guide spotlights, gear reviews. Priority Actions

Week 1-2: Activate Google Reviews
  • Request Google reviews from last 50 departing guests (immediate outreach) Create email template: "Thank you for choosing Sportsman's Cove Lodge! We'd love to hear about your experience. Leave a quick review here: [Google My Business link]." Send on checkout day. Add SMS reminder 3 days later. Offer incentive if allowed in your region (e.g., "help our lodge by leaving a review"). Assign operations person to track responses and follow up weekly. Conservative target: 10-15 reviews by end of Week 4 (20% response rate).
  • Verify and optimize Google My Business listing Ensure: Business name matches TripAdvisor/Yelp exactly ("Sportsman's Cove Lodge"), hours are current, address is correct, phone number is verified, primary website is sportsmanscovelodge.com (updated post-domain-consolidation), service categories include "Fishing," "Lodging," "Tourism." Add 5-10 high-quality photos (lodge exterior, interior, fishing boats, meals, guest testimonials). Google My Business is the gateway to Google Reviews — optimize it first.
Month 1: Build Response Culture
  • Establish TripAdvisor response protocol (check 2x per week) Assign one team member to review TripAdvisor twice per week. Reply to ALL new reviews (positive and negative) within 48 hours. Positive replies: thank guests by name, mention specific experiences they praised ("Thank you, Jane, for those kind words about Captain Tom's expertise!"), include lodge name/location. Negative replies: apologize, address the concern specifically, offer resolution, invite follow-up offline. Professional responses increase review visibility on TripAdvisor and show potential guests that you care.
  • Continue Google review generation (ongoing campaign) By Week 4, you should have 10-15 Google reviews. Continue requesting from all new departing guests. Create text message template for faster response: "Thank you for staying with us! Enjoying your experience? Quick review helps us improve: [short link]." SMS has higher response rates than email for immediate feedback.
  • Create testimonial graphics for social media reshare Design 3-5 Canva templates: Quote card (review quote + 5-star rating), Catch photo frame (guest catch + "As seen on TripAdvisor"), and Before/After carousel (lodge highlight + review). This enables fast, professional reshare of top reviews on Instagram/Facebook. Target: reshare 2-3 reviews per week from TripAdvisor/Yelp/Google.
Month 2-3: Scale & Systematize
  • Display reviews prominently on website Add "Guest Reviews" section to homepage: showcase 3-5 top reviews with 5-star ratings, link to full reviews on TripAdvisor/Google. Create dedicated "Reviews" page with embedded review widgets (TripAdvisor, Google) or mashup plugin that pulls reviews from multiple platforms. Build trust with potential bookers by making social proof immediately visible.
  • Expand review generation to past guests (email archive campaign) By Month 2, reach out to past guests who may not have left reviews. Create email campaign: "Help Other Anglers Discover Sportsman's Cove Lodge — Share Your Experience" with direct links to Google, TripAdvisor, and Yelp. Target: 5-10 reviews from existing guest database per month.
  • Monitor Yelp and respond (weekly check-in) Yelp is lower priority than Google/TripAdvisor but still visible in local search. Check weekly, respond to new reviews within 1 week, ensure business info (hours, phone, address) is current. The 29 guest photos on Yelp are underutilized — consider requesting high-res versions to feature on social/website.
