1.3 SEO Fundamentals 5 / 8 (63%)

Technical SEO, metadata, schema markup, and search visibility

1.3.1 Unique indexable location page PASS
Contact/Locations page exists at /contact-us/ with embedded Google Map, address (1061 Riverside Ave, Jacksonville, FL), and hours. Page is indexable with unique meta description.
1.3.2 Title tag: keyword + city + brand under 65 chars PARTIAL
Homepage title: "Riverside Dental | Dental Implant Dentist | Jacksonville, FL" (60 chars) — Excellent. However, sub-pages vary: Some pages have generic titles, others include service keywords but inconsistent brand mention. Service pages should use format: "[Service] Dentist in Jacksonville, FL | Riverside Dental"
1.3.3 Meta description unique <165 chars with CTA PARTIAL
Homepage has optimized meta description, but many interior pages (services, blog) lack unique meta descriptions or have generic descriptions. Need to audit and fill missing descriptions with service-specific CTAs.
1.3.4 Single keyword-rich H1 PASS
Homepage H1: "Dental Implants & General Dentistry - Jacksonville, FL" — Strong, keyword-rich, includes location and primary service focus. Service pages have dedicated H1s (Dental Implants, Cosmetic Dentistry, etc.)
1.3.5 Individual service pages for top procedures PASS
Dedicated pages exist for: Dental Implants, Full Mouth Implants, Cosmetic Dentistry, Veneers, Clear Aligners, Teeth Whitening, Root Canals, Gum Disease, Emergency Dentistry, Full Mouth Reconstruction. Excellent service page coverage.
1.3.6 Local schema markup (Organization + LocalBusiness) FAIL
No structured data (JSON-LD) markup detected for Organization, LocalBusiness, Dentist, or Service schema. Missing: business hours, phone, address, operating hours in machine-readable format. This significantly impacts local search visibility.
1.3.7 Google Search Console verification PARTIAL
Cannot verify GSC status from homepage. Recommend manual audit: Check Search Console for coverage errors, indexation issues, core web vitals, and click data. Verify property is verified and monitored.
1.3.8 Internal linking strategy PASS
Footer contains links to main pages (About, Team, Gallery, Contact, Privacy Policy). Service pages link to related procedures (e.g., Implants page links to Full Mouth Implants). Navigation structure supports crawlability and link equity flow.

Quick Wins for Section 1.3

  • Implement JSON-LD schema for Organization, LocalBusiness, and Dentist (include hours, phone, address, services)
  • Audit all sub-page title tags and meta descriptions; fill missing descriptions with service keywords
  • Verify Google Search Console connectivity; set up monitoring for indexation and Core Web Vitals
  • Add breadcrumb navigation to service pages for improved crawlability and user experience
