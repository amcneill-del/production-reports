Executive Summary

Lake Mary Dentistry demonstrates a solid foundation with a professional website featuring a prominent doctor bio, patient testimonials, and multiple service offerings. However, the site lacks dedicated service pages, has weak SEO optimization, and shows no evidence of online booking integration. The practice has strong patient trust signals (doctor credentials, long tenure since 1987, positive reviews) but needs significant improvements in conversion optimization, technical SEO, and service-line clarity to compete effectively for new patients.
