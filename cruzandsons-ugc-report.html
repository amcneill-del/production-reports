📋 UGC Resharing Best Practices

1
Always credit the creator — Tag the original poster when resharing their photo or review on social media.
2
Ask permission before feed posts — A quick DM ("Can we repost your photo?") protects the brand and builds relationship.
3
Don't edit beyond basic cropping — Keep UGC authentic. Heavy filters or edits reduce trust.
4
Engage before you reshare — Like and comment on the original post first. This builds the relationship and increases visibility.
5
Keep a UGC permission log — Maintain a simple spreadsheet of who gave permission for which content and when.
6
Thank the creator after resharing — A comment or DM saying "thanks for sharing your new fence!" strengthens customer loyalty.
