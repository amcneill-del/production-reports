2. "No Cavity Club" Certificate — Design a shareable certificate for cavity-free visits. Parents love sharing achievements. Include social handles on the certificate.

3. Review Request Automation — Send automated post-visit text with Google review link. Target: 10+ new reviews/month to address the review velocity gap.

4. "Bravery Award" Content — Create a fun Bravery Award for nervous kids who complete their visit. Parents share these moments — organic UGC generator.

5. Staff Shoutout Campaign — Encourage parents to mention staff by name in reviews (like Ms. Manny P.). Creates authentic, shareable content.

UGC Resharing Best Practices

  1. Always credit the creator — Tag the parent/reviewer and thank them.
  2. Ask permission first for feed posts — DM or comment before resharing.
  3. Don't edit beyond basic cropping — Authenticity is the point.
  4. Engage before you reshare — Like and comment on UGC before using it.
  5. Keep a UGC permission log — Track who gave consent and when.
  6. Thank the creator after resharing — Builds ongoing relationships.
  7. HIPAA note: Never share identifiable patient photos without written HIPAA-compliant consent, especially for minors.
