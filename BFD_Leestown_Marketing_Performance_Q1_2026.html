Executive Summary

Beaumont Family Dentistry — Leestown is the only BFD location with positive production growth in Q1 2026, delivering +$51,852 (+7.3%) year-over-year to reach $761,471 in gross production. The practice scorecard shows 14 of 19 metrics passing, with critical gaps in new patient call conversion (40%, benchmark 55%), missed call rate (30%, benchmark 18%), scheduling access (45-day wait for third available NP appointment — extreme bottleneck), and online reputation depth (Yelp: 6 reviews at 3.3 stars). On the clinical and operational side, the location is excelling: net patient growth of +68 is the strongest across all BFD locations, online scheduling adoption at 41.1% (72 of 175 NPs) is the highest in the organization, leakage improved dramatically (from 11.4% to 8.0%), and appointment throughput rose to 2,581 completed visits (+268 YoY). The critical constraint is capacity — the 45-day new patient wait is severely limiting growth potential. With strong execution across operations (leakage control, online adoption, net growth) and clinical delivery (same-day production, case presentation), the path forward is to urgently expand provider capacity, systematically improve new patient conversion infrastructure (call handling, missed call recovery), and capitalize on the proven online scheduling strength by making it a platform for other locations.
