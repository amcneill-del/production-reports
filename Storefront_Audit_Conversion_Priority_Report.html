Executive Summary

This report consolidates the Digital Storefront Audit findings from 32 dental and specialty practices across the SGA (18 practices) and Gen4 (14 practices) portfolios. The analysis is prioritized around converting new patients — the factors that most directly determine whether a prospective patient who visits a practice's website will actually book an appointment.

The data reveals systemic gaps across the portfolio. Only 3 of 32 practices (9%) have zero conversion-critical fails. The average Conversion Strategy score is 61%, but the average Insurance & Financing score is just 32% — meaning most practices fail to remove the financial barriers that stop patients from booking. Trust-Building averages 54%, with before/after galleries missing from 72% of all practices.

The highest-impact, most-repeatable fixes fall into three categories: (1) adding new patient offers and online booking CTAs, (2) listing specific insurance plans and adding financing/membership options, and (3) embedding reviews and before/after galleries on practice websites. These three categories alone would address over 80% of all conversion-critical fails across the portfolio.

Phased Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1: Quick Wins (Weeks 1–2)

  • Add new patient offer to every practice website (hero section + sticky banner)
  • List specific insurance plans on a dedicated /insurance page for each practice
  • Add CareCredit/Sunbit logos and apply-now links to service pages
  • Embed Google review widget on every homepage
  • Ensure click-to-call phone number is in header (mobile-first)

Phase 2: Foundation (Weeks 3–6)

  • Collect before/after photos and build gallery pages (prioritize Critical tier practices)
  • Design and launch membership/savings plans for uninsured patients
  • Add FAQ sections with schema markup to all service pages
  • Deploy Google Tag Manager + GA4 with conversion goals on all sites
  • Set up call tracking (CallRail or similar) with source-level attribution

Phase 3: Optimization (Weeks 7–12)

  • Add heatmapping/session recording (Hotjar/Microsoft Clarity) to measure user behavior
  • Optimize page speed for practices with Core Web Vitals failures
  • Add video testimonials and doctor intro videos
  • Build blog/resource sections with local SEO content
  • A/B test CTAs, offers, and landing pages based on analytics data
