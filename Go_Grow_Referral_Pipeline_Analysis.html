Executive Summary

The Go Grow referral portfolio comprises 20 baseline practices with 17 actively reporting monthly data in January and February 2026. Combined external referrals totaled 2,108 in January and 2,081 in February. Average portfolio case acceptance sits at 73.8% (Jan) and 76.3% (Feb), with time-to-consult averaging 6.8 and 4.8 days respectively — a significant improvement from 2025 baselines that ranged from 7 to 37 days.

Of the 17 tracked practices, 7 are performing well (Tier 3), 6 have targeted improvement opportunities (Tier 2), and 4 require urgent attention (Tier 1) due to declining referral volume combined with case acceptance below the 69% benchmark. Referrer yield varies dramatically across the portfolio — from 0.29 to 1.55 referrals per source per month — making it the single largest lever for growth at the practice level.
