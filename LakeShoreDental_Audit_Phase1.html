Audit Date: April 3, 2026

Executive Summary

Overall Performance: Lakeshore Dental Care has built a solid digital foundation with a professional, patient-centric website that demonstrates 30+ years of practice stability. The site successfully showcases the practice's comprehensive service offerings, experienced team, and strong trust signals (video testimonials, smile gallery, detailed service pages).

Key Strength: Exceptional trust-building assets including multiple video testimonials, comprehensive before/after smile gallery, detailed team bios with photos, and prominent new patient offers ($199 exam & X-rays, $95 cleaning). The website clearly communicates the practice's 30-year tenure and modern technology investments.

Critical Gap: Online appointment booking is missing—patients are redirected to an external scheduling link rather than having an embedded, real-time booking widget on the site. Additionally, the website lacks visible integration with Google Business Profile reviews, and some SEO fundamentals (title tags, meta descriptions, individual service page optimization) need refinement.
