Executive Summary

Peach Family Dental Group scores 24% overall (🔴 Critical). The practice's strongest asset is its doctor bio content — two detailed, authentic bios with real photos and credentials build genuine trust. However, critical gaps in conversion infrastructure (no online booking, no new patient offer, no reviews on site), analytics (zero tracking tools installed), and SEO foundations (no schema markup, no meta description, no individual service pages) are significantly limiting new patient acquisition. Multiple template artifacts from other practices (Statesboro Family Dentistry, Periodontal Associates of Memphis) indicate the site was not fully customized after launch.
