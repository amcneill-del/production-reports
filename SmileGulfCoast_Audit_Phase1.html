Executive Summary

Smile Gulf Coast demonstrates a solid digital presence with good fundamentals. The website features excellent patient-centric messaging, strong service line coverage, and prominent contact information. The practice shows particular strength in trust-building elements with comprehensive team bios and genuine testimonials. However, the site has notable gaps in conversion optimization (missing online appointment booking), SEO technical implementation, and limited mobile CTA optimization.

Overall score: 67% (Good) - The practice is above average but needs strategic improvements in online booking integration and technical SEO to compete at the highest level.
