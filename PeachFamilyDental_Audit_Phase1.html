Executive Summary & Next Steps

▼

Overall Assessment

Peach Family Dental Group has a solid digital foundation with a good website structure, strong local presence, excellent Google reviews (5.0 stars), and active social media. The practice is positioned well for growth with established service pages and clear contact mechanisms. However, there are significant opportunities to improve conversion optimization, trust-building elements, and content strategy.

Key Strengths

  • Exceptional Reviews: 5.0-star rating provides strong social proof
  • Service Page Coverage: Dedicated pages for high-value procedures (Implants, Invisalign, Veneers)
  • Location/NAP Consistency: Perfect consistency across all platforms
  • Social Presence: Active on Facebook with engaged local community
  • Team Credentials: Experienced doctors with strong patient trust indicators

Critical Gaps

  • Conversion Optimization: Weak CTAs, no time-limited new patient offer, missing financing information
  • Content Strategy: No blog, limited FAQ sections, minimal patient education content
  • Trust Elements: No visible before/after galleries, limited doctor bio details, technology not highlighted
  • Accessibility: Needs full audit for alt text, color contrast, and 404 page
  • Mobile UX: Click-to-call and CTAs may need optimization for mobile-first patients

Recommended Next Steps

  1. Week 1: Implement P1 action items (new patient offer, sticky CTA, custom 404)
  2. Week 2-3: Create Insurance/Financing page and optimize all meta data
  3. Week 4-6: Add before/after galleries, expand doctor bios, embed reviews
  4. Week 6-8: Launch monthly blog with 2-3 initial posts
  5. Week 8-12: Establish consistent social media strategy and measure results

Projected Impact (First 90 Days)
