Summary

Grade: NEEDS WORK ⚠️ 50–79%

Aiken / Augusta Oral & Facial Surgery has a solid foundation with two established locations, board-certified surgeons, and a multi-page website covering key service areas. However, the digital storefront shows significant gaps in conversion optimization, trust-building content, and technical SEO implementation that are costing the practice new patient leads.

Scoring Methodology

This audit evaluates the practice website against the Gen4 Dental Phase 1 Digital Storefront Framework, a comprehensive checklist covering 9 categories and 40+ individual items. Each item is scored on a 4-point scale:

✅ Pass (1.0 point)

Element is clearly present, well-implemented, and meets or exceeds best practices.

⚠️ Partial (0.5 points)

Element is present but needs improvement, or implementation status is unclear due to access restrictions.

❌ Fail (0 points)

Element is missing, broken, or not implemented.

➖ N/A (excluded)

Item does not apply to this practice type or cannot be assessed.

Overall Score Calculation: Total points earned ÷ Total possible points × 100 = Percentage Grade

Grade Thresholds:
80–100% = STRONG · 50–79% = NEEDS WORK · <50% = CRITICAL

Assessment Limitations: This audit was conducted using web search and third-party data sources due to domain access restrictions. Full technical audits require direct HTML/CSS access, mobile device testing, and link crawling. Recommendations for manual verification are included throughout the report. For a more comprehensive technical audit, use tools like SEMrush, Ahrefs, Screaming Frog, or Google PageSpeed Insights.
