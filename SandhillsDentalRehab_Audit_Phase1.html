Executive Summary

Sandhills Dental Rehabilitation Center has built a solid online presence with clear service positioning and professional team credentials. The website demonstrates strong specialty focus and good conversion fundamentals, earning a GOOD overall rating.

Top Strength: Excellent doctor bios with credentials, clear service line architecture, and professional design. Team member profiles enhance trust and expertise positioning.

Biggest Gap: Missing online appointment booking system and no visible patient testimonials/reviews on the website. No dedicated new patient offer or landing page. Limited blog activity impacts content authority.

Action Priority: Implement online booking integration, add patient reviews section, create new patient welcome offer, and boost content marketing.
