Audit Summary & Key Findings

Overall Assessment

Parkway Periodontics (parkway-perio.com) is a solid periodontal specialty practice with strong clinical credentials (2 board-certified periodontists) and good service line organization. However, the digital storefront has significant conversion optimization gaps that are limiting new patient acquisition and referral doctor engagement.

Strengths

  • Strong doctor credentials and experience (20+ years combined)
  • Comprehensive service offering (8+ procedures documented)
  • Multiple contact methods available
  • Mobile-responsive design foundation
  • HTTPS security implemented
  • Professional, consistent brand messaging

Critical Gaps (Conversion Killers)

  • No Online Booking: Forcing all appointments through phone/email in 2026 is uncompetitive
  • No Patient Reviews Displayed: Massive trust barrier; patients don't see social proof
  • No Before/After Gallery: Missing high-impact trust signal for cosmetic/implant cases
  • No Insurance/Financing Info: Major cost objection not addressed; lost conversions
  • No New Patient Offer: No incentive to book first appointment; missed urgency
  • No Blog Content: Zero SEO advantage, missed referral doctor engagement tool

80/20 Quick Wins (Implement First)

  1. Online Booking (Weeks 1-3): 30-40% new patient inquiry increase
  2. Google Business Profile Optimization (Weeks 2-4): 200%+ impression growth
  3. Patient Review Campaign (Weeks 2-6): 10+ Google reviews = 20-30% conversion lift
  4. Before/After Gallery (Weeks 3-6): 25-35% conversion improvement on implant pages
  5. Insurance/Financing Page (Weeks 2-3): Remove cost barrier, improve close rate
