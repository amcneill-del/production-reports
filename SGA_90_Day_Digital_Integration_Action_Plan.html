Executive Summary

This plan outlines a 90-day integration roadmap to unify 260 dental practice locations under a consistent digital standard. All 260 locations start in Field Growth (Amy McNeill, VP) and graduate into Advanced Care (Dakota, VP) once they meet rigorous criteria: digital storefront benchmarks, CRM deployment, operational readiness, and clinical approval. Target: 5–10 locations graduated in the first 90 days.

The work is organized into three phases: establishing the digital storefront foundation (websites via The Studio, GMB via Yext, social via Canva/Promo Republic), standing up scalable marketing performance reporting, and launching a 5-star patient experience program. Three operational workflows define how work gets done: website fixes route through The Studio (internal creative team), GBP optimization routes through Yext (with OM gap-fill), and social content routes through The Studio via Canva/Promo Republic.
