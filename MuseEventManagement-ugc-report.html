UGC Resharing Best Practices

1
Always Credit the Creator
Tag the author's name and company in every reshare. For testimonials, use their full name and title.
2
Ask Permission First
For feed posts, send a quick DM or email asking permission before resharing. Testimonials on your own website are already permission-granted.
3
Don't Edit the Quote
Reproduce testimonials verbatim. You may abbreviate with "..." but never change words or meaning.
4
Engage Before You Reshare
If a client posts about your event, comment and like before resharing. Build the relationship first.
5
Keep a UGC Permission Log
Maintain a simple spreadsheet of all UGC with: source, date, permission status, where it was used.
6
Thank the Creator
After resharing, send a thank you message. This reinforces the relationship and encourages more UGC.
