GOOD — Needs Work

Overall Assessment: Alpharetta Periodontics has a solid foundational website with strong specialty content, excellent doctor credentialing, and clear service offerings. However, the site is missing critical modern digital elements needed to compete in today's referral-driven perio market, particularly online booking functionality and reviews integration.

Top Strength: Comprehensive procedure pages, board certification display, team profiles, and specialty-focused navigation demonstrate expertise positioning.

Biggest Gap: No online appointment booking system (critical for referral flow), limited patient reviews, and missing Google Business Profile optimization are major conversion and discoverability barriers.

What This Means

Alpharetta Periodontics has a solid foundation but is losing competitive ground on conversion and discovery. The website clearly positions expertise but fails to capture the referral flow with modern booking tools. SEO and review deficits limit local visibility.

Without immediate action on P1 items (booking, GBP, reviews): Referring doctors will see limited availability data, patient reviews elsewhere will shape perception, and local search visibility will remain below market potential.
