Digital Presence Needs Major Work

Cruz And Sons Fence Co. scores 34% overall — placing them in the Critical range. The site has a functional foundation on Squarespace with HTTPS, a clean mobile-responsive design, an impressive project photo gallery, and a clear "Get a Free Bid" CTA on the homepage. These are real strengths to build from.

The biggest gaps are in SEO and local discoverability: the site has no title tags with keywords or city, no meta descriptions, no schema markup, no local service pages, and no Google Business Profile activity that could be confirmed. Together, these issues make it nearly invisible in local search for "fence company West Jordan" or similar queries.

Secondary gaps include missing trust signals (no reviews on site, no team photos, no privacy policy), missing conversion elements (no click-to-call in the header, no business hours, no map on the contact page), and no blog or FAQ content. The highest-priority actions can be completed quickly and at low cost — especially fixing the title tags, adding click-to-call, and claiming/optimizing the Google Business Profile.
