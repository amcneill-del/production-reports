Executive Summary

Muse Event Management's website scores 40% overall — a Critical rating that signals significant gaps in conversion infrastructure, trust signals, and technical SEO. The site has a strong visual brand and compelling service narrative, but it is not yet optimized to convert visitors into paying clients.

The biggest strength is clear, aspirational service messaging and a well-structured services architecture with multiple offering categories. The most urgent gaps are a missing primary CTA above the fold, broken navigation links (Gallery → 404), no Google Map on the contact page, no pricing transparency, no privacy policy, and critical SEO metadata failures (page title is just "Home").

With targeted fixes, this site can move from Critical to Good within one sprint. Three sections (Conversion, SEO, Trust) require immediate remediation.
