Little Smiles Pediatric Dentistry scores 56% overall — a solid foundation with several meaningful gaps that are limiting new patient acquisition. 🚨 SEO is the site's most critical weakness. The title tag is duplicated, the H1 has zero keywords, there are no individual service page URLs, and the blog is absent. These issues directly limit organic discovery of new patients searching for pediatric dentistry in Cedar Park.
